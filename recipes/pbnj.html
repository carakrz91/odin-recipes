Peanut Butter & Jelly

If you're looking for an easy lunch or snack for your toddler, look no further.

The Peanut Butter & Jelly is a classic for a reason!

With just three ingrediants, you'd be hard-pressed to find a more simple dish.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly of your choice (I prefer strawberry)
  • Two slices of bread

Steps

  1. Slice your bread (if it isn't already sliced)
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  3. Spread jelly on the other slide of bread
  4. Put the two slices of bread together and... voila!
  5. Enjoy your PB&J!
