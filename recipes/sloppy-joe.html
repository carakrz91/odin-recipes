Sloppy Joe

Summer is the perfect time for this easy and delicious dish!

Although it's great if you have a grill at your picnic, this easy meal can also be made in your regular kitchen.

The best part? I haven't met a kid so far who doesn't love it!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Ketchup or tomato sauce
  • Hamburger buns
  • Yellow mustard
  • Brown sugar

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef until no longer pink. Season with salt and pepper. Once brown, drain the grease.
  2. Add in brown sugar, mustard, and ketchup. Stir well.
  3. Reduce heat and allow meat to simmer on low for 5 minutes.
  4. Serve on hamburger buns
  5. Enjoy, but don't forget your napkins!
